About Get to know me

Hello, my name is Alexander Foerster and I enjoy learning how the internet is created. So, what’s the best way for me to do that, you ask? Well, that would be to jump in and build.

When I first started, I dabbled in digital art which led me to web design. I attended the University of Wisconsin studying Interdisciplinary Art and Technology where I graduated with honors.

Having a passion for technology and learning I got into freelance web design. I create informational websites for local small businesses by using website builders and customizing their themes using HTML and CSS.

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve been hard at work learning advanced CSS and JavaScript through autodidactic discipline. I am currently continuing my studies by learning React, Express, and Git.
